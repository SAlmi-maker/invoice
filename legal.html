JOSKA ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our car rental invoice management platform.

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data until you delete it or your account is deleted. You can delete individual invoices, clients, or your entire account from the settings page.

5. Your Rights (Morocco — Law 09-08)

Under Moroccan Law 09-08 on the protection of personal data, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
