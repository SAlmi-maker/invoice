Privacy Policy How JOSKA handles your information

JOSKA ("we", "our", "us") is a car rental invoice management platform operated from Morocco. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have under Moroccan Law 09-08 on the Protection of Personal Data. By creating a JOSKA account, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

Important: We never see your password. Firebase Authentication stores a cryptographic hash only — not even our team can read it. Your invoice data and client records are only accessible through your authenticated account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to operate and improve JOSKA. Specifically:

  • To create, store, and export your rental invoices as PDFs
  • To generate daily, monthly, and annual revenue reports
  • To display your company branding (logo, name, contact) on invoices
  • To manage your saved clients and vehicle records
  • To authenticate you securely and maintain your session
  • To manage your subscription status and enforce plan limits
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To send you important service updates (not marketing, unless you opt in)

We do not use your data to serve advertisements, build marketing profiles, train AI models, or share with analytics brokers.

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data between your browser and JOSKA is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at rest: Firebase stores your data with server-side encryption on Google Cloud
  • Authentication: Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase uses industry-standard hashing
  • Access control: Firebase Security Rules ensure each user can only access their own data
  • No employee access: Our team cannot read your invoices, client data, or financial records without your explicit consent

While we implement strong safeguards, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential. If you suspect unauthorized access, contact us immediately at support@joska.app.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the service. Here's how retention works:

  • Active account: All your data is retained and accessible as long as you are a subscriber
  • Subscription cancellation: Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation so you can export it, then permanently deleted
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account from Settings, all associated data — invoices, clients, vehicles, company profile — is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Partial deletion: You can delete individual invoices, clients, or vehicles at any time from within the app
  • Backup copies: Firebase automated backups may retain deleted data for up to 7 additional days for disaster recovery purposes

7. Your Rights — Moroccan Law 09-08

Under Law 09-08 on the Protection of Personal Data in Morocco, supervised by the Commission Nationale de contrôle de la Protection des Données à caractère Personnel (CNDP), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right of rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right of erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Export your invoices and reports as PDFs directly from the app
  • Right to lodge a complaint: File a complaint with the CNDP at cndp.ma if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@joska.app. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe a minor has created a JOSKA account, please contact us immediately at support@joska.app and we will delete the account promptly.
